# Support

Hi — glad you're here. Whatever you've got — a question, a feature idea, a bug, or just something confusing — send it to [support@x-amz.dev](mailto:support@x-amz.dev). blob is a small app and real people read the mail.

## Not sure what to include?

Don't worry about it. A one-line "this didn't work" is plenty to start. If you want to make the fix as fast as possible, these are the two things that help most:

- **A screenshot from inside the app.** Long-press a message for the share sheet, or just use the system screenshot — whatever's easier.
- **The shortcut that triggered it.** In Shortcuts, open the shortcut and hit Share → Copy iCloud Link, then paste it into the email.

Either one is great. Both is gold. Neither is fine.

## TestFlight beta
